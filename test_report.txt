Team Members Palak, Divya Singh, Arun, Krishna Pramod Dubale, Samarth Sangtani, Sarvesh Kumar
Institute Newton School of Technology

The project belongs to HR analytics and labour market intelligence. Modern hiring teams operate in a noisy market
where raw job count does not reveal whether roles are attractive, competitive, efficiently filled, or fairly
compensated. The primary decision-makers are recruitment leaders, HR business partners, workforce planners,
job-platform strategy teams, and students evaluating market opportunities.
The business value is better hiring resource allocation, improved compensation strategy, stronger candidate
targeting, and higher job-listing efficiency. A dashboard-driven system lets stakeholders move from broad market
observation to specific category, state, salary, and remote-work decisions.
